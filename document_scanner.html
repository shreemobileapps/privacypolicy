Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2024-11-05

1. Introduction

Welcome to ShreeMobileApps.

ShreeMobileApps (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates the Document Scanner app (hereinafter referred to as "Service").

Our Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data, how we safeguard your privacy, and what data we collect when you use our app. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information when you use the Document Scanner app. The app performs functions such as scanning images, text recognition (OCR), and file conversion (PDF to image and image to PDF), but all data stays on your device.

The app supports multiple languages for OCR (Optical Character Recognition), enabling text extraction in various languages. However, the text recognition process and language selection do not involve the transmission of any personal data or sensitive information. All OCR data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to external servers.

3. Types of Data Collected

Local Data Storage

The Document Scanner app stores scanned documents, images, and files locally on your device. This data is only accessible through the app and is not shared with third parties. The types of data stored may include:

  • Scanned images and documents
  • Text extracted from scanned images (OCR data)
  • Converted PDF files

Ad Data

We display ads in the app to support its development. These ads may collect information such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address
  • App version
  • Ad interactions (e.g., clicks or views)

We use third-party ad providers who may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads. However, we do not collect personal data for advertising purposes.

4. Use of Data

The data we collect is used solely to facilitate the functionality of the app and improve the user experience. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Store your scanned documents locally on your device
  • Allow you to convert images to PDFs and PDFs to images
  • Provide OCR (text recognition) for scanned documents
  • Display ads to support app development

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any of your data to third parties. All data generated and stored in the app stays on your device and is only accessible through the app itself. We do not share your scanned documents, images, or PDFs with any external parties or services.

However, when using the app's sharing features (e.g., share via email or messaging apps), the data you share will be under the control of the respective third-party services and is outside of our control.

6. Security

While we do not store any data on external servers, we recognize the importance of protecting your data. The data stored on your device is as secure as your device's security features. We recommend using a secure device password and enabling device-level security options to protect your data.

7. Ad Providers

We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you use our app. These companies may use cookies, web beacons, or other technologies to collect information about your interactions with ads to provide personalized advertising.

Some of the ad providers we may use include:

  • Google AdMob
  • Other third-party ad networks

For more information about the data collected by these ad networks, please refer to their privacy policies.

8. Data Retention

We retain the data that you generate (e.g., scanned images, PDFs, OCR data) locally on your device as long as you choose to keep it. You have full control over your files and can delete them at any time within the app or from your device storage.

2. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information when you use the All QR Code Scan/Copy/Open. Our app does not require you to provide any personally identifiable information, nor does it track or store your usage activities.

3. Data Storage

All data generated by the app, such as scanned QR codes, is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers. The information stays within the app and is not shared with any third-party services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any of your data to third parties. Any data that is stored in local storage is only accessible to the app on your device. There is no sharing of data to external servers, advertisers, or any other third-party entities.

5. Security

While we do not store any personal data, we understand the importance of security. However, as data is stored locally on your device, the security of the data depends on the security measures of your device.

We recommend that you ensure your device is protected by a secure password or other security measures to protect your data.

6. No Sensitive Data Collection

Our app does not collect any sensitive data, such as financial, health, or personal identification information. The only data handled by the app are the QR codes you scan and any content directly associated with the QR code, which is stored on your device.
